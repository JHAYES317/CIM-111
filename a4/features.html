Aventura Mall

Aventura Mall is the third largest mall in the U.S. and the largest mall in Florida.

Turnberry Isle

Turnberry Isle is a luxury resort with golf courses, tennis courts, restaurants, and a spa. The golf course contains three miles of walking paths and lakes near millions of dollars worth garden displays and high-rise residential apartments, and overlooks the Atlantic Ocean where famously the Yacht Monkey Business was featured in the story about Gary Hart and Donna Rice which ended his 1988 presidential run.
