Aventura Mall

Aventura Mall is the third largest mall in the U.S. and the largest mall in Florida. It holds over 300 stores, 50 different restaurants, and 20 museum pieces through the experiential arts program.

Turnberry Isle

Turnberry Isle is a luxury resort with golf courses, tennis courts, restaurants, and a spa. The golf course contains three miles of walking paths and lakes near millions of dollars worth garden displays and high-rise residential apartments, and overlooks the Atlantic Ocean where famously the Yacht Monkey Business was featured in the story about Gary Hart and Donna Rice which ended his 1988 presidential run.

Aventura Arts and Cultural Center

The Aventura Arts and Cultural Center is an ornate new age 330 seat venue for the performing arts and cultural events set on the waterfront of Aventura. It is sponsored by the Broward Center for the Performing Arts.

Founders Park

A tribute to the founders of aventura in 1995 is featured on a monument inside the 12-acre Founders Park, which is divided into north and south sections. The north section has a 1/4 mile running trail, a playground, 3 tennis courts, and an sports field. The south side features the Aventura Community Green Garden.
